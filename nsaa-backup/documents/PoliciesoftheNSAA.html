NSAA Policies

Failure to pay fines 

Members who do not comply with the policies below, will need to pay fines, and members who don't pay their fines have three options:

  • Pay their fines like a normal person
  • Serve their sentence (1 neck = 1 wildcash)
  •  Have their membership suspended until they pay their fine.

 

No necking

Part 1 - Types of Necking

  • Neck - any type of physical contact on the neck.
  • Throat - Any type of physical contact on the throat.
  • Chin - when someone tries to Throat you but they hit your chin.
  • Shrek - A Throat and Neck at the same time.
  • Scissor neck - When someone forms scissors with their arms, and yeets the sides of your neck.
  • Punch - when you get punched.
  • Slap - when you get slapped.
  • Poke - a mini stab.
  • Slice - when someone slices you.
  • Death - When someone hits you so hard, you literally die.
  • Kick - When someone kicks you with their leg.
