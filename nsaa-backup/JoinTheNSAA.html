Join The NSAA

Welcome to the NSAA!

There are five ways a member can join:

 

1.   Website

1.         Go to This Link

2.         Click on the new member form

3.         Complete the form

 

2.   Short Url

1.         Go to https://tinyurl.com/nsaajoin

2.         Complete the form

 

3.   QR Code

1.         Ask an NSAA member for a QR code or go here

2.         Complete the form

 

4.   Email

1.         Send an email to nsaa.reports@outlook.com with “join” as the subject

2.         Wait for an automatic reply

3.         Click the reply

4.         Click on the form link

5.         Complete the form

 

5.   Manually (For people without internet or a device)

1.         Ask an NSAA member for a form (may not be available - most likely not)

2.         Complete the form
